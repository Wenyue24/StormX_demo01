StormX (Zurich demo) Interactive Demo

StormX transforms observed rainfall into climate-adaptive 2D rainfall fields, Intensity–Duration–Frequency (IDF) curves, and design-storm ensembles for robust flood-risk assessment and resilient infrastructure planning.

Built from station, radar, and satellite observations, StormX provides current and future rainfall products for stations, points, catchments, and user-defined areas. Engineers, insurers, municipalities, and planners can directly use these outputs in flood modelling, drainage design, infrastructure planning, and climate adaptation applications.
